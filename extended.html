Two Year Program Endurance

‘Endurance’ is a two year intensive program ideal for students studying in class XI. The students will get an ample amount of preparation in these two years, which if utilized properly, can give a sure shot selection in their dream courses.

Salient Features -

Concept Builder and Special Classes - Maximum hours of concept building and specialised classes offered by experienced faculties.

Number of Hours – 600+ Hours

Personalized/ 1-on-1 Mentorship Program – Gradual mentoring of students to monitor progress and devise individual action plan to improve result and performance.

Specialized Sessions – Specialized and focused classes to boost your preparations.

Targeted Current Affairs classes covering all important updates around the globe.

Special vocabulary booster sessions – Learn & memorize (for lifetime) more than 1000 new words in a fun and interactive use of mnemonics.

Special exam-oriented classes for all major exams like IIM IPMAT, CLAT etc.

Specialized Sessions for Written Ability Test (WAT)

Interactive Online Learning Portal – We offer best in class PRATHAM Student Information System (PSIS). Each student will have access to this portal where he/she can utilise the following features –

Video Lectures - Get access to recorded lectures of all the classes & exam specificQA, VA, GA, LR and CA. Learn the concepts at your own pace anytime, anywhere and also practice the questions based on it with shortcut methods. A must have item in your inventory.

Form Notification and Updates – Regular alerts regarding colleges/universities admission dates and criteria, changes in the pattern of exams, updates of upcoming forms are provided.

Form Filling assistance - Regular blogs and constant support to help students crack the exams. Also, get Form Filling Videos to help you in filling of forms and have a hassle-free preparation and experience.

Exam Pattern and Analysis – Get access to all the information related to various entrance exams. The file covers every detail like pattern, number of seats, last year analysis etc. that will surely be of a great aid.

Faculty-on-demand – Unique feature of “Ask a query directly from the faculty” further adds value in your preparations.

Performance Report Card – A summary of all of your attempted mock test and quizzes. Helps in tracking your performance.

Form Wallet – A secured & efficient structure to store all your details related to forms, passwords and results.

Academic Quizzes - Further practice questions from each and every topic of a subject to get a grasp on it more effectively.

250+ additional Practice Tests for proper practice

Benchmark your performance with top rankers and improve. One who utilizes this efficiently is guaranteed to perform well in these entrance examinations. The same level of analysis will also be provided for Offline tests.

Design Your Own Test (DOT) – Unique feature where you can create your own test related to any subject to ensure 100% perfection in a particular topic. Endless practice at your disposal.
